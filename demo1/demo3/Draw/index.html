Your Payment Options

Your E*TRADE Bank offers flexibility in making your monthly interest payments: ACH,check or drawing on yourline of credit.Principal payments are not required as long as your line is in good standing. Good standing means that the balance outstanding is less than 70% of the value of the collateral supporting the line. You can make prepayments on your line of credit at ant time without penalty.

1.ACH

Our preferred method of payment is electronically by ACH.ACH payments are the lowest cost,most reliable and safest methods of making a payment - which is why we recommend them for you.Within the ACH option you also have the flexibility to make a ont-time pay ment,set up an automatic payment to pay the interest due each month, or make arecurring fixed payment amount.Toset up ACH payments,click here to be directed to our secure payment site.

3.Draw on your Line Credit

If you elect to make your monthly interest payments payments by drawing against your Line Credit,you are adding the monthly interest payment to the outstanding principal balance.The added interest. The amount drawn against your Line Credit wi;; be the monthly interest payment due as indicated on your monthly statement. This option is also the default option.Any interest not paid by the due date will automatically be paid by drawing on your Line Credit.Payments received after the due date will be applied to your outstanding principal balance.
